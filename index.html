Aurale Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 8, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Aurale. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains that our app is designed in a way that no data is collected, stored, or processed.

No Data Collection

Aurale does not collect any personal information, usage statistics, or technical data. We do not have any mechanisms—intentional or accidental—for tracking your behavior or storing your data. In short, we do not collect any data.

  • Personal Information: We do not request or store your name, email address, or any other personal details.
  • Usage Data: No information is gathered about how you interact with our app.
  • Technical Data: We do not collect IP addresses, device identifiers, or other technical details.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our app and this website do not use cookies or any other tracking technologies. There is no data being stored on your device for tracking or analytics purposes.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any third-party services that collect or process data on your behalf. Your interaction with Aurale is completely private.

Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no sensitive information that could be compromised. We have designed our app and website to be as secure and privacy-friendly as possible.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Although we do not collect any data, we reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
