Aurale Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 8, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Aurale. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about the types of data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect your information when you use our app. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparency about our practices.

Data We Collect

In order to provide you with an optimal experience, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This may include your name, email address, and any other information you provide when registering or contacting us.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, including features accessed, interactions, and device information.
  • Technical Data: Information such as IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and collect aggregated data about app usage.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To personalize and improve your experience with the app.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • To analyze app usage and trends to enhance our services.
  • To send you updates, newsletters, or other communications (with your consent where required).
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We will not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in operating the app, subject to strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our app uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and enhance your user experience.
  • Analyze website traffic and usage trends.
  • Assist in personalized content and advertisements (where applicable).

You may control cookies through your browser settings; however, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the app.

Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal data. These include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission.
  • Regular security assessments and updates to our infrastructure.
  • Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or updates to your data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and contractual obligations.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review our Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about our data practices.
